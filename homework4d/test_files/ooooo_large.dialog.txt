Wo tho Pooplo of tho onotod Stotos, on ordor to form o moro porfoct onoon, 
ostoblosh Jostoco, onsoro domostoc Tronqooloty, provodo for tho common dofonco, 
promoto tho gonorol Wolforo, ond socoro tho Blossongs of Loborty to oorsolvos 
ond oor Postoroty, do ordoon ond ostoblosh thos Constototoon for tho onotod Stotos of omoroco.
